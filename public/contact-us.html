Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

What is the typical response time for support inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, we recommend using our live chat on Telegram or Skype for immediate assistance. Our 24/7 support team is always ready to help.

How can I become a publisher or advertiser?

You can sign up directly on our website by clicking the "Sign Up" button. For publishers, we require at least 5,000 daily visitors. For advertisers, there's no minimum budget requirement. Our team will review your application within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We support multiple payment methods including Wire Transfer, PayPal, Payoneer, WebMoney, and Bitcoin. Publishers receive weekly payments with a minimum payout threshold of $50. Advertisers can fund their accounts using any of these methods.

Do you offer technical integration support?

Yes! Our technical team provides full integration support for all publishers. We offer detailed documentation, code snippets, and one-on-one assistance to ensure smooth implementation of our ad codes on your website.

What countries do you support?

We support publishers and advertisers from all countries worldwide. Our network covers 200+ countries with tier-based pricing. We accept traffic from any geography and work with partners globally.

How can I track my campaign performance?

Our platform provides a comprehensive real-time dashboard with detailed analytics. You can track impressions, clicks, conversions, revenue, and other key metrics. Custom reports can be generated and exported at any time.
