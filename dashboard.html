Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I create a campaign?
To create a campaign, click on the "Create Campaign" button from the dashboard or campaigns page. Fill in the required details including campaign name, budget, target URL, and targeting options. Once submitted, your campaign will be reviewed and activated.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure Stripe payment gateway. All transactions are encrypted and secure.
How is my budget used?
Your campaign budget is used to pay for clicks or impressions based on your bidding strategy. You can set daily or total budgets, and campaigns will automatically pause when budgets are reached.
Can I pause or edit my campaigns?
Yes! You can pause, resume, or edit your campaigns at any time from the Campaigns page. Changes to targeting and budgets take effect immediately.
How do I get a refund?
Unused funds in your account balance can be refunded at any time. Please contact our support team with your refund request, and we'll process it within 5-7 business days.
What kind of support do you offer?
We offer 24/7 email support and live chat during business hours. Our team typically responds within 2-4 hours. For urgent issues, please use the priority support option.
